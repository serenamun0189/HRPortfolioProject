HR Dashboard Project

This project demonstrates an HR analytics dashboard built in Power BI, analyzing employee performance, job satisfaction, and attrition across departments and job roles.


Dashboard Screenshot

![Image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9fc9cb53-ba74-4ca5-b4b0-f5fd5f0643cd)

> The screenshot above shows key metrics such as department-level attrition rates, average performance ratings, and insights into job satisfaction versus income and distance from home.

Department-Level Metrics: Average performance rating and attrition rate.  
Job Role Analysis: Attrition rates by role and job satisfaction comparison.  
Income and Distance Insights: Categorizes employees by income tier and distance from home to analyze job satisfaction trends.  
Aggregations & Grouping: Used `AVG`, `SUM`, and `CASE` statements to calculate metrics.
